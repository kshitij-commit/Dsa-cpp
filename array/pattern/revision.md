## Q01 - Decreasing Prefix

Pattern

3 13 12 7 4
3 13 12 7
3 13 12
3 13
3

Learning

- Outer loop controls rows.
- Inner loop starts from index 0.
- Inner loop ends at n-i.


## Q02 - Increasing Starting Index

Pattern

3 13 12 7 4
13 12 7 4
12 7 4
7 4
4

Learning

- Outer loop decides starting index.
- Inner loop always prints till the last index.
- Inner loop starts from j = i.
